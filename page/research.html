Research

Evolutionary Computation

I am currently studying evolutionary computation. This is mainly because I often use genetic algorithms to solve constrained optimization problems in data modeling competitions. I really want to join Professor Claus Aranha's research group. Because he is so humorous and the content of his homepage is very attractive to me. I think it must be a very cool thing to join them.

I am currently studying the decomposition and automatic combination of meta-heuristic algorithm components. I hope to combine new algorithms for a class of problems through offline training. I hope to try various methods such as evolutionary computing, machine learning, etc. for this combined training. At the same time, I plan to make it my undergraduate graduation project.
